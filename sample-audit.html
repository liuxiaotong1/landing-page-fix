Executive summary

The page explains the system before it earns interest in the outcome.

  • 1

    Replace category jargon with the job the buyer wants done

    “Data-collection scenarios” is accurate, but it makes visitors decode the category. “Forms that send clean data to your workflow” is concrete.

    High impact
  • 2

    Choose the first buyer before listing every possible use case

    Teams, founders, marketers, developers, agents, webhooks, analytics, and future automation appear together. Lead with one urgent use case.

    High impact
  • 3

    Make the CTA promise an immediate result

    “Generate form” is functional. “Build my first lead form” reduces ambiguity and matches the suggested prompt directly beneath it.

    Quick win
