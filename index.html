Questions, answered

Before you hand us the URL.

Is this generated by AI?

AI assists with analysis and organization, but this is not an instant auto-generated score. Your page is checked against your actual audience and conversion goal, then the recommendations are edited and prioritized specifically for the page.

When does the 24-hour clock start?

It starts once payment and the required brief are received: the page URL, target buyer, and primary conversion goal. If the page is inaccessible or the brief is incomplete, delivery pauses until the missing access or information arrives.

What is the refund policy?

Before work begins, you can request a full refund. After delivery, contact us within 7 days if the review is not specific and actionable; we will revise it once or refund it. Full details are in the service terms.

What kinds of pages are a fit?

SaaS homepages, product landing pages, waitlists, and launch pages with one clear conversion goal. Large e-commerce catalogs are not a fit for this offer.

Can you implement the changes?

Yes. After the teardown, a fixed-price implementation option is available for simple HTML, React, Next.js, or copy-only changes.

What if my page is not live yet?

A public Figma prototype or shareable staging URL works. The review needs enough content to understand the offer and intended action.
